I am a lead research scientist at Telefónica Research in Barcelona, Spain. Previously, I was a member of technical staff at Nokia Bell Labs in Dublin, Ireland. I received my Ph.D. from University College Dublin (UCD), Ireland. Before joining Telefónica Research, I worked as lead data scientist at Telefónica Alpha, where I later helped launch two startups that spun offs: I co-founded Bia Power and worked as senior data/applied scientis at Koa Health. I have also held roles including Senior Data Scientist at Intel Labs in Dublin and Principal Data Scientist at Sanofi in Barcelona.

Focus

My research interests span networking and distributed systems, with an applied machine learning lens. I focus on measurement-driven, carbon- and energy-aware cloud/edge and mobile/edge systems, including mobility-aware MEC and data-driven network intelligence.
